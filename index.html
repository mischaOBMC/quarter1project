About Djerf Avenue

Djerf Avenue is a sustainable fashion brand founded by Matilda Djerf, characterized by its commitment to timeless, high-quality designs. The brand emphasizes eco-friendly practices, using sustainable materials and ethical production methods to minimize environmental impact. It focuses on sustainable and timeless clothing, offering a curated selection of pieces that embody a relaxed, Scandinavian aesthetic.
